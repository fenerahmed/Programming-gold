AboutClimate change, simply put, is the change in temperature and weather a place experiences over a period of time. The difference between climate and weather is that climate is over long periods of time, and includes information such as rainfall, wind patterns, rising sea levels and other things, the most well-known measurement is the average surface temperature. All of these factors determine how the climate is like in a region. Over decades, the average surface temperature in most parts around the world have been increasing at an alarming rate according to NASA's website. The website just really puts into perspective the crazy rises in global temperature. The impacts of climate change are but not limited to, more severe storms, increased droughts, rising in ocean levels and damage to ecosystems.
